Get Familiar with XPeditionary Gear

Your safety and comfort are our top priority, and that starts with the gear you'll be using on your adventure.

At XPeditionary, we equip you with only the highest-quality, certified gear designed to keep you safe and enhance your performance.

Whether you're paragliding through the skies or scaling a rock face, our gear is your first line of defense.

Why Our Gear is the Best

We source and use only the best equipment available, ensuring that each piece meets the highest safety standards.

Our gear is designed for both performance and comfort, giving you peace of mind during your adventure.

Here's why our gear stands out:


  • Certified Safety Standards: All of our equipment meets or exceeds international safety standards, including CE and UIAA certifications.
  • Durability: Built to withstand the toughest conditions, ensuring longevity and reliability on every adventure.
  • Comfort & Fit: Designed for a snug, comfortable fit that minimizes distractions and enhances your XPerience.
  • Regular Maintenance: Our gear is regularly inspected and maintained to ensure it's always in top condition.

Gear for Your XPedition

Each Xtreme sport requires specialized gear. Below is a list of the high-quality equipment you'll use for different activities:


Paragliding Gear

Our paragliding gear includes harnesses, helmets, gloves, and safety jackets—all designed for comfort and safety.

Our certified paragliding gear ensures maximum stability in the air, with adjustable harnesses and parachutes that meet the highest standards.

Rock Climbing Gear

For rock climbing, we provide durable climbing shoes, harnesses, carabiners, ropes, and belay devices.

All our gear is made from high-strength materials and is thoroughly tested to meet international climbing standards.

Kayaking Gear

Our kayaking gear includes top-of-the-line paddles, life jackets, and wetsuits.

Whether you're kayaking in calm waters or rushing down a river, our gear is designed to keep you safe, comfortable, and agile.

Canyoning Gear

For canyoning, we provide wetsuits, helmets, ropes, and harnesses designed to protect you as you navigate waterfalls, rocky terrain, and swift rivers.

Our gear ensures that you can safely XPlore even the most challenging environments.

Gear Maintenance & Care

We understand how important it is to have reliable and safe equipment. That's why all our gear undergoes regular maintenance, inspections, and replacements.

Our dedicated team ensures that every piece of equipment is cleaned, checked for wear and tear, and tested for performance before being used by any client.

We're committed to providing you with the best gear for your XPedition, and we go the extra mile to ensure it's always in peak condition.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We believe in protecting the environment while providing the best gear for your Xtreme sports adventures.

We source our equipment from manufacturers who prioritize eco-friendly materials and sustainable practices.

Additionally, we recycle and properly dispose of old gear, ensuring minimal environmental impact.

How to Use Our Gear

Each piece of gear we provide comes with detailed instructions for use, including safety tips, proper fitting techniques, and maintenance advice.

Our XPert guides will walk you through the correct usage of all equipment before your activity begins, ensuring you're comfortable and ready to go.
