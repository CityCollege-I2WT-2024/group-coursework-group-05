The developers met in September 2024 as first-year Computer Science students at CITY College, University of York Europe Campus. Their shared passion for technology, creativity, and problem-solving quickly brought them together. When faced with a challenging group project for the Introduction to Web Technologies module, they teamed up to bring their ideas to life. XPeditionary is the result of their collaboration and marks their first major project as a team.
