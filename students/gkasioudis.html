Who Am I?

I am Ioannis Gkasioudis, a 19-year-old college student from Athens. I am passionate about exploring the limitless world of gaming and technology. Since my childhood, I have been fascinated by the creative process behind video games and their ability to transport players into immersive, fantastical worlds. My dream is to become a game developer and create experiences that leave a lasting impact on people.

I am currently pursuing my first degree in Computer Science, laying the foundation for a career in game development. With a strong interest in programming, storytelling, and design, I aim to harness my skills and creativity to contribute to the gaming industry in meaningful ways.


My Passions

Gaming has been a significant part of my life, inspiring me to dive deep into game mechanics, storytelling, and design. My favorite genres include RPGs, strategy games, and platformers. Playing games like The crew, Cyberpunk 2077, and Elden ring has fueled my desire to create my own worlds.

Beyond gaming, I enjoy coding, exploring new technologies, and learning the fundamentals of game engines like Unreal Engine and Unity. These tools have allowed me to experiment with interactive environments and build small projects.

When I am not glued to my computer, I love reading fantasy novels, listening to music, and watching anime series. These hobbies inspire my creativity and keep me balanced.

The Journey Ahead

This website was built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. HTML creates the structure, organizing the content into sections like the header, main content, and footer. CSS handles the design, styling the layout, buttons, images, and text for a polished and responsive look. JavaScript adds interactivity, such as buttons to cycle through images for each activity and a dark mode toggle. Additionally, I built separate pages like Air.html, Earth.html, airDestinations.html, and earthDestinations.html to provide detailed information about different activities and destinations, ensuring a comprehensive and user-friendly experience.
