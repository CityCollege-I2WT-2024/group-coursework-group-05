Panos Katsigiannis


Who???

I was born in 1979, in Thessaloniki. I have been an odd one ever since. I have always enjoyed exploring the different aspects of life and myself, while trying to maintain a curious and open-minded outlook, often through unconventional paths. Over the years, I have pursued diverse fields and activities, gaining formal education in music and business management, while working in roles ranging from musician and music teacher to hotel receptionist, lifeguard, cost control manager, and project officer.

Through these experiences, I have come to see adaptation and communication as the most vital functions of all systems, whether in nature, society, or technology. Perception shapes how we adapt, and language underpins how we communicate. This realization has kept me a committed student of life and the human condition, always eager to learn and grow.

As I observe the exponential impact of computers and AI on human history and the future of our civilization and aesthetics, I see these tools as collaborators â€” partners that have added new gears to the machinery of progress. In my view, being literate in the era that is dawning means understanding and harnessing the language of computers and AI. This understanding has driven me to pursue a first degree in Computer Science at CITY College, University of York Europe Campus, where I am beginning a journey into a field that has fascinated me for years.


What the Cat???


To know a person is to know what they love and how they spend their free time.

My two great loves are animals and music. I have been fortunate to share my life with several pets, who are as much a part of me as my family. Music has been a constant companion since I started playing the piano at 8 years old. Over the years, I have sung and played keyboards in various blues-rock and hard-rock bands, sharing moments of passion and creativity through live performances.

Books, movies and anime have also been cherished companions, offering endless stories and perspectives to explore.

I enjoy staying active through martial arts, such as judo and ninjutsu, while I am always up for a game of basketball. Physical exercise helps keep my mind sharp and my body energized.

One of my favorite pastimes is gathering with friends to play Dungeons & Dragons, a tabletop role-playing game where we immerse ourselves in stories as heroes embarking on impossible quests to meet and overcome monstrous challenges.


The Project - Resources

The website you are visiting was developed using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, leveraging modern front-end technologies to ensure a responsive and visually appealing design. Key techniques included the use of the <"meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0"> tag for responsiveness, the CSS Flexible Box Layout (display: flex;) for layout consistency, and properties such as border-radius, box-shadow, and transform to enhance the aesthetic appeal of icons, links, and images.
